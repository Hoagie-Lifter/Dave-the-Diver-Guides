Dave the Diver - Cave System Guide

Area Overview

The Cave System in Dave the Diver is a complex and intricate underwater maze filled with unique marine life and hidden secrets.

Fish Species

Cave Dwelling Fish

Depth: 50-150m

Spawn Time: Varies

Notes: Adapted to dark environments

Blind Cavefish

Depth: 100-200m

Spawn Time: Constant

Notes: No eyes, uses echolocation

Fishing Tips

  • Bring multiple light sources
  • Navigate carefully through narrow passages
  • Watch for hidden currents

Challenges

The Cave System presents unique obstacles:

  • Maze-like structure
  • Limited visibility
  • Unpredictable water flows
