Dave the Diver - Shallow Reef Guide

Area Overview

The Shallow Reef is the first area you'll explore in Dave the Diver. It's characterized by crystal clear waters and a diverse range of marine life.

Fish Species

Blue Tang

Depth: 5-10m

Spawn Time: Early Morning

Notes: Common and easy to catch

Clownfish

Depth: 3-7m

Spawn Time: All Day

Notes: Often found near coral formations

Fishing Tips

  • Use small nets for smaller fish
  • Move slowly to avoid scaring fish away
  • Pay attention to depth and time of day

Challenges

The Shallow Reef presents unique challenges:

  • Limited visibility in some areas
  • Fast-moving currents
  • Aggressive territorial fish
